Bringing Golden Age Radios Back to Life

We specialize in the complete restoration of vintage radios from the 1930s through 1960s. Every dial, tube, and cabinet receives meticulous attention to preserve both beauty and authentic sound.

The Restoration Process

1. Evaluation

Comprehensive assessment of your radio's condition, original specifications, and restoration needs.

2. Disassembly

Careful removal of chassis, documentation of wiring, and preparation for restoration work.

3. Electronics

Replacing aged capacitors, testing tubes, cleaning contacts, and ensuring electrical safety.

4. Alignment

Precision alignment using period-appropriate test equipment to restore optimal performance.

5. Cabinet Work

Veneer repair, wood restoration, refinishing with authentic methods and techniques.

6. Final Testing

Extended testing period, final adjustments, and comprehensive quality assurance.
